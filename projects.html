RICE Preparations

RICE Preparations was an event planning business which was created in order to allow minorities to feel more represented. In doing so, this business would host an event which would promote local minority owned restaurants to help them grow as a business and to help them feel more represented in their own community.

Bloom Box

The Bloom Box is a box which allows students to become more engaged in STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Art, Math) while strengthening collaboration skills by working with teams. The Bloom Box would not only make the environment pop, they can also be proud that they took part in the process of potentially helping create our next generation of engineers. This project was awarded the SM Wilson Centennial Award in 2021.
